assignment : 3D model of a potential semester project

I decided to change my final project because I am also a musician I really wanted to do something music related. And this is what I came up with.

Musicvision
A light structure controlled by tones. A different light color for each note/frequency ( A-B-C-D-E-F-G) and the strenght of the light is controlled by the pitch. The aim is to make live music more visual and ejoyable for the hear impaired.
